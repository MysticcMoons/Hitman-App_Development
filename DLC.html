Roles and Gamemodes

Roles

➢Murderer: Your goal is to eliminate all innocents while staying undetected. ➢Civillian: No special roles, main task is to stay alive and vote out the murderer. ➢Henchman: Protect the murderer at all costs. Protecting their reputation, alibi, and not limited to eliminating yourself. - Don’t get too comfortable, the murderer can dispose of you. ➢Guardian Angel: This ability can only be used if you are dead. Protect the life of one player. - This is a one time use ability. ➢Jester: Act Suspicious and convince others to vote you out. If you are voted out, you win the game. ➢ Doctor: One time revive on any eliminated player found mid round. - Undead: Revived by the doctor, you CANNOT talk, signal, or contribute to any voting process. Once you are deemed “undead” you lose all of your abilities. YOU ARE JUST A BODY. Special Roles ➢ Mayor: Can reveal their role publicly to gain extra voting power. (e.g their vote counts as three). - VETO: Take the dice and roll, if you land on #1-2, you can completely veto a vote. If it lands on #3-6, you are impeached and lose your voting power. This can only be done if you have not used your ability. ➢Conjoined Twins: Link arms with the player to your left for the entirety of the game. If you or your twin are separated you are eliminated. If you or your twin are murdered the surviving twin continues the game with the deceased twin physically linked. - Once your conjoined twin has been eliminated they assume the role of “undead” . ➢Cupid: Immediately reveal your role publicly and choose two “love birds” . If either of the selected players are eliminated, the other must die of "immediate fatal heart break”
